This app helps you schedule meetings or events with others by creating a simple date poll.

How it works:

  1. Create a poll by entering a title and choosing possible dates.
  2. Share the poll link with others.
  3. Each participant selects their preferred dates and enters their name.
  4. You can view the final results with names and votes side by side.

No account or login needed. Just simple, fast scheduling.
